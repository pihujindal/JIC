About JECRC Incubation Center (JIC)

JIC – JECRC Incubation Centre (Jecrc foundation) at Jaipur empowers student innovators and early stage startups with mentorship, co-working space, and seed funding. Our dynamic ecosystem has supported 80+ student-led startups and incubated 150+ ventures to date, providing resources to launch ideas into viable businesses. We invite young founders to join JIC – apply now to access workshops, incubator space, and investor networks.

Our Programs

Whether you are preparing to put your idea into action or want to scale your early-stage startup, we have tailored programs just for you. JIC provides mentorship, support, and funding opportunities to empower student entrepreneurs to turn their ideas into impactful ventures.

Induction 1.0

Induction 1.0 is an engaging learning module where students are introduced to the complete content creation journey—how to shoot videos, how to edit them, and how to use AI tools effectively. It equips students with practical skills, creativity, and digital confidence, enabling them to transform their ideas into professional-quality content.

Induction 2.0

Induction 2.0 focuses on building strong technical skills, offering students hands-on training in web development, Salesforce, application development, and other essential technologies. It helps learners understand real-world tech workflows and equips them with industry-relevant abilities. This module empowers students to transform ideas into functional digital solutions with confidence and competence.

Accelerator Programs

Our Virtual Accelerator and 6-week accelerator programs help budding entrepreneurs (especially JECRC students) validate concepts. This includes intensive mentor sessions, MVP development, and demo days. Selected teams even receive seed funding and pitch practice.

Incubation Program

Incubation Program: Graduates of our accelerator enter a full-time incubation with co-working desks, labs, and industry networks. We guide founders through business model refinement and investor readiness. The incubation program connects closely with our accelerator so founders can smoothly “level up” – from ideation to prototype to market (see Apply Now for eligibility).

• Pre-Incubation (Ideation & Bootcamps)

We run hackathons, ideation workshops, and our Khoj Startup Conclave to spark ideas. For example, the annual KHOJ Yatra brings 40+ startups, investor panels, and pitch contests to campus, promoting entrepreneurship in Tier‑2/3 regions.

Mentorship & Workshops

Weekly sessions like Leader’s Talk series and mentor office hours connect innovators with entrepreneurs, corporates (AWS, Microsoft for Startups, IVCA partners, etc.), and government leaders. Founders gain advice on fundraising, market strategy, and product design from experienced mentors.
