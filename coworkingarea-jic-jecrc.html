Our Coworking Facilities

JECRC Incubation Centre offers an ecosystem designed for collaboration and creation. From flexible work areas to advanced labs and industry connections — JIC ensures that every startup has the right foundation to grow.

Coworking Space & Amenities

JIC’s 50+ seat coworking space offers up to four desks per startup, enabling teams to collaborate in a dynamic, professional environment. Equipped with high-speed internet, meeting rooms, event spaces, and a shared pantry, the space is designed to inspire productivity and community.

Industry & Pilot Project Access

JIC provides exclusive investor connect rooms and pilot-test access through its industry and government partnerships. Startups can organize trial runs and pilot projects in domains like smart manufacturing and rural innovation — leveraging JECRC’s strong industrial collaborations.
